# Membria EE (Enterprise Edition)

Membria EE is designed for enterprise environments with strict compliance, data residency, and security requirements.

## Who it is for
- Enterprises with regulated data
- Teams that require private knowledge domains
- Organizations with custom compliance workflows

## What you get
- Dedicated deployment options
- Private Council and cache controls
- Enterprise governance and auditability
