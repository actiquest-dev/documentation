> This is a short starter guide for Membria CE. It will expand as onboarding and release packaging are finalized.

## Steps
1. Choose Membria Cloud or waitlist for self-hosted access.
2. Connect at least one source (drive, chat export, or docs).
3. Run an initial ingestion and review the Decision Surface.
4. Verify your first Decision Black Box captures and confirm or dismiss signals.
