## Pricing tiers

| Tier | Target | Users | Price | Features |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Starter | Small firms | Up to 25 | 2,000 USD per month | Core Decision Surface, DBB, 4 system agents |
| Professional | Growing firms | Up to 100 | 5,000 USD per month | Custom agents, integrations, analytics |
| Enterprise | Large firms | Unlimited | Custom | On-prem, SSO, dedicated support, SLA |

## Key metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Decisions captured | Briefs created per month | 50 plus per firm |
| Precedent reuse | Percent of decisions referencing precedents | Over 60 percent |
| Time to precedent | How fast users find past decisions | Under 30 sec |
| Drift detection | Inconsistencies flagged per month | Baseline plus trend |
| User adoption | MAU divided by total users | Over 80 percent |
