# Membria for SMBs (SaaS)

Membria for SMBs is a managed SaaS offering for teams that need shared context, decision continuity, and fast onboarding.

## Who it is for
- Small and mid-sized teams
- Cross-functional groups with shared decisions
- Teams that need governance without enterprise overhead

## What you get
- Shared GraphRAG and decision capture
- Team-level LoRA scopes
- Workspace controls and audit logs
