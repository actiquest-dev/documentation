# Decision Brief Builder (DBB)

## Overview

Decision Brief Builder is the structured decision documentation tool. It creates a decision brief that becomes part of institutional memory.

## When it is used

- A partner makes a client decision
- A committee reviews an exception
- A team closes an engagement and documents learnings
- A new precedent is created for future use

## Decision brief structure

| Section | Purpose | AI assistance |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Decision | Clear statement of what was decided | Butler suggests wording |
| Context | Background and client situation | Auto-extracted from docs |
| Reasoning | Why the decision was made | Butler prompts for completeness |
| Alternatives | What else was considered | Butler suggests common alternatives |
| Approvals | Who signed off and when | Workflow automation |
| Documents | Supporting materials | Auto-linked from discussion |
| Precedents | Related past decisions | Butler finds automatically |

## AI features

**1) Precedent finder**
- Surfaces similar past decisions
- Shows match percentage and similarities
- One-click to view the full precedent

**2) Reasoning completeness check**
- Butler prompts for missing elements
- Ensures institutional standards are met

**3) Discussion integration**
- Chat is attached to the brief
- Attachments from chat are auto-linked
- Butler participates in the discussion

**4) Precedent creation**
- One-click "make this a precedent"
- Butler suggests categorization
- Future decisions reference it automatically
